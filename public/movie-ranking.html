The best movies according to Emmanuel

My top 3 movies of all time

The dark knight

Christopher Nolan's approach humanizes Batman, showing his internal struggles and the psychological toll of his vigilante lifestyle, making him a more relatable hero.

The Matrix

The core concept of the Matrix, where humans are unknowingly living in a simulated reality controlled by machines, raises profound questions about free will, reality, and the nature of consciousness.

The terminator

The idea of a machine sent back in time to assassinate a target before they are born was a novel and unsettling concept that captured the imagination of audiences.
